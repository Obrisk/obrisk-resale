Introduction

Obrisk is a software project based upon the use of advanced web technologies to provide robust solutions to challenges of sharing and connection that are lacking in other widely used social networks. Currently, we are focusing on foreigners living in China, giving them a powerful platform where they can share, relate and help one another to conquer different problems that foreigners in China face, mostly because of language barrier and a wide range of cultural differences.

Obrisk was founded in 2018 by a very small team of passionate software developers in China. We are working so hard to make this platform the best place ever in the entire internet. This project is still under development and we will truly appreciate any feedback, suggestions or other contibutions.
